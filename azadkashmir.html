Azad Kashmir

Azad Kashmir is the Pakistan-controlled region of Kashmir. The region is comprised of mountainous valleys, lakes, and rivers. Due to the difficult geography and limited field data, remote sensing models and products can be utilized to understand the characteristics of the region for variables such as temperature, Precipitation and vegetation.
